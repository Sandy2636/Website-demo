Ingredient Story

1. FRUITS

Fruit is in the DNA of Kiga ice cream. It’s what makes our ice creams unique and original. We go to great lengths to source fresh fruits only from their most popular locations. Fruits that are freshly plucked from farms eventually end up in freshly served scoops of delicious Kiga ice cream.

2. MILK

Milk is a key ingredient in our ice cream. The milk used comes from the same trusted vendors we’ve partnered with for years. This ensures every scoop is consistently rich, creamy, and satisfying.

3. SUGAR

We use pharma-grade sugar that’s so pure it’s certified for medical use. It ensures hygiene and perfect sweetness, making every scoop safe and delightful.
