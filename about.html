Our Journey

From our very first scoop to becoming a beloved brand across Maharashtra, this is our story.

2017 - The Beginning

KIGA started with a vision to deliver nostalgia and delight through carefully crafted ice creams. Our humble beginnings featured basic flavors and refreshing cold coffees that warmed every heart.

2018 - First Outlet

We proudly launched our first outlet in Hadapsar, Pune, offering 18 handpicked flavors. The customer response was overwhelming and drove our desire to grow further.

2020–2021 - Rapid Growth

Even during uncertain times, we expanded to 24 outlets across Maharashtra. Innovation like Ice Cream Tacos helped us stand out and delight customers.

2022 - Trust & Innovation

With over 170 franchise inquiries, we knew we had built something special. We introduced tubs, cones, and snack formats to expand our reach and serve smiles in more ways.

Today - 65+ Flavors and Growing

We continue to delight ice cream lovers across Maharashtra with 65+ handcrafted flavors. Every scoop reflects our commitment to purity, taste, and innovation.
