Our Story

Kiga Foods brings you the authentic taste of Indian traditional ice creams, crafted with passion and premium ingredients.

Tradition Meets Innovation

Founded in 2010, Kiga Foods began with a simple mission: to bring authentic Indian ice cream flavors to every household while preserving traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Today, we combine time-honored techniques with modern technology to create premium ice creams that capture the essence of Indian culinary heritage.
