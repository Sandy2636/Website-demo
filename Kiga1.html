Our Story

Founded in 2017 in Pune, Kiga Foods began as a small ice cream shop with a passion for creating exceptional frozen treats using only the finest natural ingredients.

What started as a single location has grown into a beloved brand across Maharashtra, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while expanding our offerings to include premium ice creams, cold coffee drinks, and delicious snacks, all made with the same dedication to quality that defined our beginnings.
