Years of Experience

Our Scoop Story

Founded in 2013, Kiga Ice Cream began as a small family-owned shop with just six flavors. Today, we've grown into a beloved local institution, but we've never lost sight of what makes our ice cream special.

We still make every batch by hand in small quantities, using milk from grass-fed cows and seasonal fruits from nearby farms. No artificial flavors or preservatives—just honest, delicious ice cream.

Visit us and taste the difference that care and craftsmanship make in every scoop.
