Our Story

From Tradition to Innovation

Kiga Foods was born from a passion to bring authentic Indian flavors to the world of premium ice cream. Our founders, Kiran Suresh Salunke and Ganesh Rajendra Gosavi, combined their expertise in dairy technology and traditional Indian recipes to create something truly special.

We use only the finest ingredients, from Alphonso mangoes to real Kashmiri saffron, ensuring each scoop delivers an unforgettable taste experience.
